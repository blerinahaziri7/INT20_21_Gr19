About Us

Launched in 2019, SELENE's architecture unique strategy has established it as the go to source of inspiration and influence across architecture and design. Our curatorial expertise and award-winning approach to storytelling is unparalleled. We work with exceptional talent, and both established and emerging architects.In the years since, we have worked on a wide array of unique projects with some of the best albanian architects. We strive to keep our work unique and smart, with room for a little magic. You can find us anywhere, and if the project is the right fit, we’d love to work with you.
